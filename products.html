From Insights to Innovations

The packaging you choose must meet environmental goals, stakeholder expectations and product performance —all at once. At Sonoco, that balance is what we’ve delivered for well over a century.  

As a global leader in sustainable paper and metal packaging, our designs offer impact, efficiency and end-of-life solutions.  

From snack and canned food containers to protective tubes, fiber components, and industrial reels, our product portfolio knows no bounds. The purpose is Better Packaging for a Better Life, a key driver for our business and the people who help build our solutions.

Saco malla

Rigid Paper Containers provide a lightweight, sustainable packaging solution for snacks and powders, helping your brand stand out on the shelf. Built for performance and on-brand precision, our paper and plastic cartridges provide clean, durable, mess-free dispensing.  

Saco malla Plus

Durable and endlessly recyclable, our metal cans for food, specialty and aerosol products preserve freshness, extend shelf life and protect your product with confidence. 

Piola

High-performance tubes and cores, paper-based materials and custom adhesives for the toughest demands of global industrial markets— textiles and film, corrugated and recycled paperboard and construction-related activities. 

Saco tejido

From automotive parts to foodservice smallware and protective cores, our custom plastic solutions combine precision engineering with industry-leading innovation. 

Servicios

We’re more than a packaging company—we’re one of the world’s largest recyclers, using what we collect to design and test innovative solutions that drive real impact for our customers and communities. Additionally, In-house engineers and scientific specialists deliver comprehensive design and testing solutions that meet your requirements.  

Servicios

Markets

Elevate your product with value-added packaging. With more than 125 years of packaging experience, Sonoco’s capabilities and expertise support your brand-specific needs. Serving various markets, we leverage our strategically located global operations to advance sustainability, maximize efficiency, ensure consistent quality and more. 

Automotive

Spray degreasers, lubricants and cleaners without making a mess. Sonoco’s aerosol technology ensures precise spraying for automative products. 

Beauty & Personal Care

Build, remodel or renovate with reliable products. Sonoco’s high-quality adhesive, sealant, caulk, concrete, flooring board, spray paint and other construction solutions help projects remain on time and within budget. 

Construction

Build, remodel or renovate with reliable products. Sonoco’s high-quality adhesive, sealant, caulk, concrete, flooring board, spray paint and other construction solutions help projects remain on time and within budget. 

Food & Beverage

Enjoy tasty foods and beverages from recyclable containers. From stacked chips to baked beans, Sonoco’s sustainable paper and metal cans preserve freshness and maintain quality. 

Household Products

Take care of the home with trusted packaging. Whether unboxing an appliance, misting air freshener or unrolling carpet, Sonoco’s paper and metal solutions protect and preserve products from plant floors to front doors. 

Industrial Paper Producers

Elevate and protect your industrial brand with Sonoco’s consistent paper products. With a centrally-managed global network, we build in contingency and create reliable products that keep production running.  

Pet Care

Protect pet health and well-being with Sonoco—a member of the Pet Sustainability Coalition. Our metal wet pet food cans and paper pet treat and snack containers not only preserve product quality but also enable recycling after use.
